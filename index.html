    What is Work Notes?

  • Hi, I’m Dave. I’ve been freelancing for ten years.

    Work Notes is a collection of articles written by me that explore freelance and self-employment topics like money, contracts, clients and well-being. It’s where I share and document my real life freelance experiences.

    Most articles appeared on my web design blog first, but I’ll be expanding the collection as time allows.
